# Business Sample

## Before

Digital transformation enables organizations to improve operational efficiency, enhance customer experience, and create new business models. Companies that adopt digital technologies are better positioned to compete in dynamic markets.

## After

Digital transformation is not only a technology upgrade; it often changes how a firm operates, serves customers, and captures value. Companies that modernize core workflows and use data effectively may respond faster in shifting markets, though the gains depend on execution quality rather than adoption alone.

# Social Science Sample

## Before

Social media use has become an important factor in shaping political participation among young adults. Online platforms provide new opportunities for civic engagement, information sharing, and collective action.

## After

For many young adults, political participation now develops partly through online spaces. Social platforms can lower the barrier to civic discussion, circulate political information quickly, and help loosely connected groups coordinate action. Still, the effect is not automatically positive; platform design and social context matter.
